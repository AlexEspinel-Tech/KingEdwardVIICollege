    • About us

    King Edward VII College, founded in 2010 and based in Melbourne CBD, offers nationally accredited programs in management, marketing, human resources, and international business. With around 500 students from Australia and abroad, the College combines high-quality teaching with practical, industry-relevant skills.
    Known for its competitive fees and innovative learning methods, the College delivers strong student satisfaction and successful graduate outcomes. As part of its growth, two new campuses will open soon in Brisbane and Sydney, expanding access to quality education while maintaining its core values of innovation and leadership.
